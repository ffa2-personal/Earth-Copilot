# Wildfire & Smoke Response — West Texas Logistics

**Facility scope:** `tx-log-lubbock-01`, `tx-pkg-elpaso-01`, and any TX site downwind of an active wildfire.

## Trigger criteria

Activate when **any** of the following is observed:

- NWS / NIFC fire-weather watch or red-flag warning over a county within 50 mi.
- 24-hour PM2.5 forecast > 35 µg/m³ at the facility.
- Active wildfire perimeter within 25 mi.

## Operational impacts

| Risk | Threshold | Action |
|------|-----------|--------|
| Outdoor staff exposure | AQI > 150 | Move yard ops indoors; issue N95 to required outdoor staff. |
| HVAC particulate load | PM2.5 > 35 µg/m³ | Switch to MERV-13+ filtration; double filter-change cadence. |
| Truck routing disruption | Highway closure within 50 mi | Reroute through northern I-40 corridor; notify customers. |
| Power line risk | Red-flag warning | Coordinate with Oncor / Xcel for proactive de-energization plan. |

## Lubbock yard (`tx-log-lubbock-01`)

- Pre-position water tankers at the yard during multi-day red-flag warnings.
- Confirm fuel reserves before any forecast wildfire week (refueling may be curtailed).

## El Paso packaging (`tx-pkg-elpaso-01`)

- Coordinate with cross-border partners in Juarez on smoke-day staffing windows.
- Pause outdoor packaging staging if PM2.5 > 55 µg/m³.

## Stand-down

- 48 hours after AQI < 100 sustained and no active perimeter within 50 mi.
